Connect Wallet to CollabLand in PizzaDAO Discord to Claim Role

Follow these steps to connect your wallet and get your role, skip this steps if you already have Box or Pizza Mafia role:

  1. Go to the #collabland-join Channel:
    • Open Discord and navigate to the PizzaDAO server.
    • Find the channel named #collabland-join.
  2. Connect Your Wallet:
    • Look for our Mafia Bouncer bot asking you to connect your wallet.
    • Click on the connection prompt or type /connect if available.
    • Choose your wallet (like MetaMask) and confirm the connection on your wallet app.
  3. Get Your Role:
    • After connecting, the bot will check if you own a 'Rare Pizza Box' or 'Rare Pizza NFT'.
    • If you do, you'll automatically get the 'Box Mafia' or 'Pizza Mafia' role.
Note: Make sure you're connecting to the official bot to avoid scams.
